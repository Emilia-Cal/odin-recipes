Bell Pepper Rice

Bell Pepper Rice is a light and easy to prepare meal, you can also add lots of different vegetables to the dish to spice it up a bit.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small sweet onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small red bell pepper, chopped
